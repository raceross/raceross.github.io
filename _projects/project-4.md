In the summer of 2023 I volunteered for a week at EAA AirVenture Oshkosh on behalf of the Right Footed Foundation, presenting the RV-10 automated door project alongside a University of Arizona student whose capstone project was a set of foot operated flight controls covering ailerons, elevator, rudder, and throttle. Throughout the week I spoke with attendees about both projects, solicited donations for the Right Footed Foundation's mission, and helped demonstrate the foot controls on a flight simulator. Notable interactions included presenting to a Delta Airlines executive and engaging with several aviation content creators. The experience gave me a firsthand look at how accessible aviation technology is received by the broader general aviation community, and reinforced the real world demand for the kind of engineering work the projects represented.
