As part of a small team at Timberline Automation and Control Co., I assembled and commissioned bottle deposit systems for recycletek, a company supplying automated return units to charities and businesses throughout the San Francisco Bay Area as part of California's bottle deposit initiative. The systems (EcoPod, EcoCenter, and EcoHub) range from bag-only deposit containers to fully automated units with integrated bottle drop and can/bottle counting hardware, with configurations spanning grid power, battery, and solar setups. Working primarily as a two person team, I performed all mechanical and electrical installation, taking bare modified containers and trailers and building them into field ready units. I also provided DFA/DFM and quality assurance feedback throughout the process to inform ongoing design improvements. Of the roughly ten units completed and deployed, the performance in the field contributed to recycletek being awarded multiple grants through California's bottle deposit program.
